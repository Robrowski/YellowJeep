Welcome to Yellow Jeep.

Yellow Jeep is the new repository facilitating the three biggest try-hards in RBE 3002: Matt, Rob and Tim. The following sections explain the current major branches.

The projects worked on by Team Yellow Jeep will mostly use ROS

Master Branch

This is the currently the main branch, holding only one ROS package, beginner_tutorials. It also holds useful bash scripts and someday fully implemented packages created in RBE 3002.

Website Branch

The website branch is actually "gh-pages", which holds the website you are looking at now!

Lab Branches

As team Yellow Jeep drives through the term, separate branches may be created for individual labs. Once labs are completed and polished, they may be merged into the Master Branch.
